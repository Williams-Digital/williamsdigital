Things to Do

Activities

From woodland hikes to live blacksmithing — there's something for every visitor.

Outdoors

Outdoor Recreation

Explore 1,500 acres of trails, creeks, and natural beauty.

Hiking

Scenic woodland trails wind along Roupes Creek, perfect for all skill levels and beautiful in every season.

Fishing

Cast a line in the cool waters of Roupes Creek for a peaceful morning or afternoon on the bank.

Biking

Ride through designated trails in the park — great for both casual rides and more challenging routes.

Picnicking

Spread out at one of our designated picnic areas — ideal for families and group gatherings.

Camping

250+ campsites with options from full hook-ups to primitive settings deep in the woods.

Nature Photography

Stunning creek-side views, historic structures, and abundant wildlife make for incredible shots.

History

Historical Attractions

Step back in time and explore the remnants of Alabama's iron-making past.

Iron & Steel Museum

Browse artifacts and exhibits that document the region's industrial heritage, from early furnace operations to the Civil War era.

Tannehill Furnaces

Explore the towering remnants of 19th-century iron furnaces and coke ovens — the heart of the park's historical significance.

Grist Mill

Tour a working grist mill and learn how grain was ground in the 1800s. A unique hands-on look at early American industry.

Blacksmith Demonstrations

Watch skilled blacksmiths forge iron using traditional techniques. Iron pour events are held throughout the year at the Vulcan Forge.

Family

Family Fun

Miniature Railway

Take a ride on the miniature railway — a family favorite that delights visitors of all ages.

Tannehill Creamery

Cool off with ice cream, drinks, and snacks after a day of exploring the park.

Crafts Cabins

Visit artisan workshops and see traditional crafts being made — from woodcarving to dulcimer building.

Plan Your Visit

Open 365 days a year, sunrise to sunset.
