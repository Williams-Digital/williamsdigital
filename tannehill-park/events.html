Upcoming

2026 Events

Mark your calendar for these popular park events.

March 7, 2026

Dulcimers Meeting

Monthly gathering of dulcimer players and enthusiasts. Bring your instrument or just come to listen and enjoy the music.

March 7, 2026

Tannehill's Living History

Step back in time with demonstrations of Civil War-era life, camp activities, and historical reenactments throughout the park.

March 14, 2026

Woodcarvers Demonstration

Watch talented woodcarvers create intricate pieces using traditional tools and techniques. A great experience for all ages.

March 14–15, 2026

ACA Bike Race #5

Competitive cycling event through the park's scenic trails. Spectators welcome — come cheer on the riders.

March 21–22, 2026

Tannehill Trade Days

Our signature event! Browse handcrafted goods, antiques, local art, food vendors, and live entertainment across the park grounds.

Spring 2026

Easter Egg Hunt

Annual family-friendly Easter celebration with egg hunts, games, and springtime activities for children of all ages.

Year-Round

Recurring Events

These events happen regularly throughout the year.

Trade Days

Held monthly, featuring dozens of vendors with crafts, antiques, food, and live music. One of Alabama's best-loved markets.

Civil War Reenactments

Living history events bring the 1860s to life with period camps, demonstrations, and battle reenactments.

Car Shows

Classic and custom car shows draw enthusiasts from across the region for a day of vintage automobiles and community fun.
