Our Mission

Celebrating the
Ford Mustang

The Mustang Museum of America is dedicated to the celebration, preservation, education and display of the Ford Mustang, providing value to visitors from the general public, members of the Museum and other automobile hobbyists.

As a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit, we are committed to preserving automotive history for future generations.
